Introducing MobiGarage - A B2B Platform for Electronics, Spares and Accessories

Introducing MobiGarage – Retail Technology Platform for Electronics, Spares & Accessories MobiGarage is a leading retail technology platform designed to empower businesses across the electronics ecosystem, including mobiles, accessories, and spare parts. The platform enables Tier 2, Tier 3, and emerging market retailers to digitize operations, manage inventory efficiently, and access the best pricing for their products. By connecting a wide network of phone retailers and mobile kiosks, MobiGarage addresses the common gaps in stock availability and pricing in the unorganized electronics market. It enhances the accessibility of high-quality, affordable products for end customers and facilitates smooth liquidation of electronics, spares, and accessories, strengthening India’s circular economy.
